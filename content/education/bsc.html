In early 2003, I started to be involved in research by participating in monitoring and undergraduate research. During this period I focoused my studies on fluid dynamics and finally presented my bachelor project {%- endmark %} entitled "Atmospheric Boundary Layer Simulation in Wild Tunnel" where we had analyzed experimentally the effects of fluid flow and its boundary layer over a tropical and dense forest. This project had been started after some issues of local measurement and thus the experimental simulation in wind tunnel was performed to understand the atmospheric conditions in the Amazon Rainforest. After this project I received the mechanical engineer diploma.
